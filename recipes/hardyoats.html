Hardy Oats

Eat Big Lots for many Energies

This recipe will get you jacked, plain and simple

A go-to for cinnamony goodness!

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Brotein Powder
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Almond Milk
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Add 2 Cups Oats into Bowl
  2. Add Almond Milk
  3. Add Brotein
  4. Add Cinnamon
  5. Add Vanilla Extract
  6. Add Butter
  7. Stir
  8. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes
  9. Add Chocolate Chips
  10. Enjoy!
