Sandwich

A healthy, filling meal that will keep you full for hours!

This recipe will keep you moving for hours! Easy, simple to make and wonderful to eat!

I generally eat this about once per day.

Ingredients

  • Ezekiel Bread
  • Turkey
  • Garlic Spread
  • Mayo
  • Spicy Brown Mustard
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Spinach
  • Cheese
  1. Griddle Bread
  2. Broil Turkey to melt Cheese
  3. Apply Sauces
  4. Apply Turkey
  5. Cut Tomato and Onion
  6. Apply Tomato and Onion
  7. Add Spinach
  8. Cut in Half
  9. Enjoy!
